The Legacy

Since 1927, the Arshad family has transformed raw gemstones into heirlooms. Each piece is a dialogue between old-world craftsmanship and contemporary soul.

Our atelier in Jaipur and flagship on Fifth Avenue carry the same ethos: no stone is set without intention, no gold alloyed without reverence. We invite you to hold a story.
