Heritage & Craftsmanship

For over a century, Arshad Jewelers has stood as the guardian of a legacy—one that fuses the intricate geometry of Islamic art with the timeless allure of pure gold. Our ateliers, nestled in the heart of the old city, are a sanctuary where master artisans breathe life into precious metals, drawing inspiration from Mughal palaces, celestial patterns, and the poetic elegance of calligraphy.

Each piece is a dialogue between past and future: hand-engraved motifs echo the starlit tessellations of Alhambra, while the warmth of 22-karat gold reflects the desert sun. We do not merely craft jewellery; we preserve a language of adornment passed down through four generations. Trust, purity, and an unyielding commitment to detail define every creation—from a solitaire ring to an opulent bridal set.

Our philosophy is rooted in the belief that true luxury whispers rather than shouts. It is felt in the weight of a perfectly balanced bangle, seen in the soft glow of an emerald cabochon, and carried in the confidence of those who wear our art.

— The House of Arshad
